Sample Data for Responder Report

Heavy rainfall caused flooding along Camalaniugan-Santa Ana Road, affecting multiple households and vehicles. 
The water level reached up to 1.5 meters in low-lying areas. 
Immediate evacuation and road closure were necessary to ensure public safety.

- Evacuated affected residents to safe locations.
- Placed warning signs and barricades along the flooded sections of the road.
- Coordinated with local authorities to provide temporary shelters.
- Documented the situation with photographs for reporting.
